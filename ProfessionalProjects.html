Big Foot Hunter

In "BigFoot," explore a chilling world of choices and consequences amidst the ancient trees of a mysterious forest. Follow Emily, a lost woman seeking escape, as she stumbles upon an isolated cabin. Inside, three inhabitants, Samuel, Eliza, and Caleb, reveal the horrifying truth—the presence of a fearsome creature known as Bigfoot, drawn to Emily's stranded car. Panic ensues as Bigfoot crashes through the door with a spine-chilling howl, leaving the group in disarray. Players must decide to either embody the deadly Bigfoot, seeking revenge on the trespassing humans, or unite as humans, equipped with specialized tools like the Bigfoot thermal tracker, animal traps, and deadly mines to hunt down the elusive monster..
