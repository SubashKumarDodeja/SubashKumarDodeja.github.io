Color Basket

Color Basket is a hypercasual game developed in Unity. The objective is simple yet challenging: guide the colorful ball into the corresponding colored basket. To achieve this, you must tap the right side of the screen to make the ball jump towards the right, or tap the left side to make it jump towards the left.
